They say it takes a village to raise a child - but we believe it takes a workshop!

At KMW, we’ve had tremendous success working with kids from single-parent families or adoptive arrangements. Our residential setup means we can offer respite care for as long as it takes, giving parents and guardians a much-needed break and allowing the kids to socialize and have fun with a supportive, creative group.

Every adult remembers what it was like to be a kid, but every kid is different. Trauma such as bereavement at a young age can call for years of intensive support, which may be more than any single caregiver can provide. KMW helps relieve that burden and the guilt that comes with it.
