About KMW

The Kids’ Media Workshop (KMW) is a unique non-profit organization that helps kids by channeling their creativity.

Rooted in a foundation of child psychology, our mission is to give troubled kids a healthy outlet for their thoughts and feelings - by producing great entertainment for other kids!

Our supervised skills sessions give kids the tools they need to express themselves through writing, animation, and voice acting.
